# Service Pricing Framework

## Pricing Model

Home service pricing is typically based on:
1. **Lot size** (square footage of serviceable area)
2. **Service type** (mowing, aeration, fertilization, cleanup, etc.)
3. **Frequency** (weekly, bi-weekly, one-time)
4. **Difficulty modifiers** (slope, obstacles, access, distance)

## Per-Square-Foot Pricing Template

| Service | Base Rate (per sqft) | Min Price | Notes |
|---------|---------------------|-----------|-------|
| Weekly Mowing | $0.005 - $0.008 | $35 | Recurring customers only |
| Bi-Weekly Mowing | $0.006 - $0.010 | $45 | Higher per-visit due to overgrowth |
| Spring Cleanup | $0.008 - $0.012 | $75 | One-time, includes debris removal |
| Fall Cleanup | $0.010 - $0.015 | $85 | Leaf removal intensive |
| Aeration | $0.004 - $0.006 | $60 | Annual service |
| Overseeding | $0.003 - $0.005 | $50 | Often bundled with aeration |

## Lot Size Estimation

When exact measurements aren't available:
- **Small residential:** 2,000 - 4,000 sqft
- **Medium residential:** 4,000 - 7,000 sqft
- **Large residential:** 7,000 - 12,000 sqft
- **Extra large:** 12,000+ sqft

Preferred: use satellite measurement tools (DeepLawn, Google Maps area tool) for accurate sqft.

## Difficulty Modifiers

| Factor | Adjustment |
|--------|-----------|
| Steep slope (>15 degrees) | +15-25% |
| Heavy obstacles (trees, beds, play equipment) | +10-20% |
| Limited access (no gate, narrow path) | +10-15% |
| Distance from service area center | +$5-15 per trip |
| Overgrown/first cut | 1.5-2x base price |

## Estimate Generation Workflow

1. Receive lead with address
2. Measure lot size (satellite tool or customer-provided)
3. Identify requested services
4. Calculate base price: sqft x rate
5. Apply difficulty modifiers
6. Apply minimum price floor
7. Round to nearest $5
8. Generate estimate document
9. Send via email template within 1 hour of receipt

## Pricing Rules

- Never price below the minimum. Minimum covers fixed costs (drive time, equipment wear).
- Recurring customers get the base rate. One-time jobs carry a premium (if offered at all).
- Bundle discounts: 10% off when combining 3+ services in a single visit.
- Seasonal pricing: spring cleanup and fall cleanup are premium (high demand, labor intensive).
- Always quote the full-season price, not per-visit, when discussing annual agreements.
