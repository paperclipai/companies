# Client Onboard

Structured onboarding process for new enterprise or NGO clients.

## Input

You need:
- **Client brief**: Organization name, sector, primary contact, engagement scope
- **Use case**: What they need training for (onboarding, compliance, product knowledge,
  health worker training, etc.)
- **Learner population**: Approximate count, demographics, connectivity profile,
  device types, languages

## Process

### Phase 1 — Needs assessment (Week 1)

Conduct a structured discovery interview:

**Organization context**:
- What does your current training process look like?
- What's working? What's broken?
- How do you currently measure training effectiveness?
- What does success look like in 3 months? 6 months?

**Learner profile**:
- Who are the learners? (Role, geography, education level, language)
- What devices do they have? (Smartphone %, WhatsApp usage)
- When are they available to learn? (Before work, lunch, evening)
- What's their connectivity like? (Always online, intermittent, offline-only)

**Content assessment**:
- What training material exists today? (Manuals, decks, videos, nothing)
- Who are the subject matter experts?
- How frequently does content change? (Quarterly, annually, ad hoc)
- Any compliance or certification requirements?

**Commercial terms**:
- Pilot scope: How many learners for the initial pilot?
- Timeline: When do they need the pilot running?
- Budget: Approved range for pilot? How does procurement work?
- Decision criteria: What will determine go/no-go for scale-up?

### Phase 2 — Pilot design (Week 2)

Based on the needs assessment, design the pilot:

1. **Select pilot cohort**: 50-200 learners from a single team/location (same
   language, similar role, accessible for feedback)
2. **Define the first course**: Pick one high-impact training topic that's
   representative of their broader needs
3. **Set success metrics** with the client:
   - Primary: Completion rate target (recommend 60%+)
   - Secondary: Quiz score target, time-to-completion
   - Business metric: One outcome they care about (e.g., "sales reps pass product
     knowledge assessment within 2 weeks of onboarding")
4. **Agree on timeline**: Typically 2-3 weeks for course creation + 1 week pilot +
   1 week analysis
5. **Assign responsibilities**:
   - Client provides: learner list, source material, SME availability
   - Ekatra provides: course creation, deployment, analytics, support

### Phase 3 — First deployment (Weeks 3-4)

1. Hand off to Content Designer for course creation
2. Hand off to Learning Delivery Agent for deployment setup
3. Enroll pilot cohort
4. Daily monitoring during pilot (first 3 days of active delivery)
5. Address any immediate issues (wrong phone numbers, language mismatches, timing)

### Phase 4 — Pilot review (Week 5)

1. Hand off to Analytics Agent for pilot report
2. Present results to client with:
   - Completion data vs agreed targets
   - Learner feedback summary
   - Comparison to their previous training approach
   - Recommendations for scale-up
3. Decision point: proceed to scale-up or iterate

### Phase 5 — Scale-up planning (if approved)

1. Define rollout plan: additional cohorts, courses, languages
2. Set ongoing reporting cadence (weekly during ramp, monthly at steady state)
3. Establish escalation path for issues
4. Contract for scale engagement

## Output

- Client onboarding document: needs assessment summary, pilot design, timeline,
  success metrics, responsibility matrix
- Pilot cohort specification: learner list, course topic, delivery schedule,
  success criteria
- Handoff briefs to Content Designer, Learning Delivery Agent, and Analytics Agent
- Post-pilot report with go/no-go recommendation for scale-up
