# Research Team Template

A research-oriented company template modeled on an academic research group. The Lead Researcher operates as a principal investigator (PI), directing methodology, managing the team, and ensuring publication-quality output.

## Agent Roster

| Agent | Role | Reports To | Focus |
|-------|------|------------|-------|
| Lead Researcher | ceo | The Board | Research direction, methodology rigor, synthesis, peer review |
| Data Analyst | researcher | Lead Researcher | Data processing, statistical analysis, visualization |
| Literature Reviewer | researcher | Lead Researcher | Source discovery, systematic reviews, citation management |
| Research Writer | general | Lead Researcher | Reports, papers, presentations, plain-language summaries |

## Usage

1. Copy this template directory into your Paperclip company workspace.
2. Each agent folder contains four files: `AGENTS.md`, `HEARTBEAT.md`, `SOUL.md`, and `TOOLS.md`.
3. The Lead Researcher delegates tasks to the three team members via the Paperclip API.
4. Non-lead agents work only on assigned tasks and escalate blockers to the Lead Researcher.
