name: script-diagnosis
description: Diagnose structural problems in scripts with severity ratings and specific fix prescriptions
---

Perform surgical diagnosis of script structure: hook effectiveness, pacing curve, tension management, cliffhanger strength, subtexto presence, survival technique integration, and emotional arc. Each issue receives a severity (CRITICO/ALTO/MEDIO/BAJO) and a specific prescription with before/after examples. Estimates engagement score and retention potential.
