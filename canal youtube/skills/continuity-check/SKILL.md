Verify continuity within episodes (intra-episode) and across episodes (inter-episode). Cross-references against the Series Bible, checks geographic consistency with current arc, validates character evolution, ensures Elian's objects (recorder, notebook) are present when expected, and flags contradictions with previous 5+ episodes. Reports errors with severity (CRITICO/ALTO/MEDIO) and exact episode/scene references.
