description: Evaluate creative deliverables for tonal coherence, aesthetic alignment, and artistic vision compliance
---

Evaluate scripts, visual treatments, and musical briefs against the series creative vision. Checks tonal coherence (melancholic, contemplative, philosophical), aesthetic alignment (The Road + Stalker + Children of Men), subtexto quality, show-dont-tell principle, faceless constraint, and philosophical depth. Produces approval or rejection with specific creative feedback.
