---
name: council-session
description: Convene and moderate Creative Council sessions with 5-perspective evaluation and majority/minority synthesis
---

Convene Creative Council sessions where 5 senior directors evaluate deliverables from distinct perspectives: Artistic Vision, Narrative Craft, Lore/Continuity, Visual/Cinematic, and Standards. Each perspective produces an independent evaluation. The President synthesizes into a majority/minority recommendation, benchmarks against excellence references (Breaking Bad, True Detective, The Last of Us), and archives decisions as precedents.
