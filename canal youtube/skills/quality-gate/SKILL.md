---
name: quality-gate
description: Standardized quality evaluation with PASS/NEEDS_WORK/FAIL verdicts and actionable feedback
---

Execute standardized quality gate evaluations at each phase of the production pipeline. Default posture is NEEDS_WORK. Each evaluation produces a checklist with item-level verdicts, specific feedback with section/asset references, and an overall PASS/NEEDS_WORK/FAIL decision. Maximum 3 rejection cycles before escalation to next hierarchy level.
