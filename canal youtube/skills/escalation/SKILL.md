---
name: escalation
description: Escalation protocol for quality gate failures with structured handoff to next hierarchy level
---

Manage escalation when quality gates fail after 3 rejection cycles. Follows the escalation ladder: Specialist -> Department Lead -> Director -> Showrunner-CEO -> Human. Each escalation includes full context (what was rejected, why, what was tried), options for resolution, and recommended path forward. Human escalation is reserved for final approval, budget, and strategy decisions.
